Crash-safe hook setup for this build
===================================

What changed:
- UI/Scaleform hooks remain enabled.
- StageResourceHooks are compiled but disabled by default.
- ScenarioHooks are compiled but disabled by default.
- SceneHooks are compiled but disabled by default.
- All hook addresses used by the new hook layer are centralized in:
  OnlineMenu/Hooking/UniversalHookAddresses.h
- Address scan reports were generated in:
  OnlineMenu/Hooking/AddressScanReport.md/json/csv

Why:
The last crash happened after broad Resource/Scenario hooks attached. Those functions are hot
startup paths, and the detour signatures/logging are not proven safe yet.

Next test:
1. Build steam_api64.
2. Launch the game.
3. Check that the log shows only FlashBridge hooks as [Hook OK].
4. If stable, enable one group at a time in HookGroups.cpp.

Re-enable order:
1. SceneHooks only.
2. StageResourceHooks with DatabaseLookup still commented/removed.
3. ScenarioHooks last, one hook at a time.
